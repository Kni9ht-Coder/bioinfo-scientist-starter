---
name: literature-audit
description: Use for literature checking, citation verification, and paper cards.
---

Rules:
1. Never invent citations.
2. Every reference needs DOI, PMID, PMCID, arXiv ID, bioRxiv/medRxiv DOI, or official URL/accession.
3. Classify each source as supports, contradicts, background, or method reference.
4. Do not cite a paper unless the claim matches it.

Output paper cards under literature/paper_cards/ and verified BibTeX in literature/bib/library.bib.
