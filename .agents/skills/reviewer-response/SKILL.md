Do not promise experiments not performed. Separate completed changes from planned changes.

For internal review loops:
1. Use docs/internal_review.md for manuscript structure issues.
2. Use docs/overclaiming_audit.md for claim and wording risks.
3. Use docs/figure_review.md for figure mirroring and clarity notes.
4. Use docs/reproducibility_audit.md for reproducibility gaps.
5. Treat automated review as triage; human scientific judgment remains final.
