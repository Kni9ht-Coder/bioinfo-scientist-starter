---
name: idea-novelty
description: Use for generating, ranking, and auditing bioinformatics or AIDD paper ideas before experiments begin.
---

Workflow:
1. Restate the candidate idea.
2. Identify the biological problem and computational contribution.
3. Identify closest prior work and novelty risk.
4. Identify public datasets and feasibility.
5. Score novelty, feasibility, publishability, reproducibility risk.
6. Reject or reframe ideas requiring unavailable wet-lab validation.

Output: docs/idea_novelty_report.md and update docs/decision_log.md.
